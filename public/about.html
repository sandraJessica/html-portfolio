About Me

My name is Sandra Jessica Wittek. Born in Prien am Chiemsee in Bavaria, I grew up surrounded by the scenic landscapes of Grabenstätt and Winkl, near the beautiful lake Chiemsee. Nowadays, I call Ismaning, close to Munich, my home.

I began my career with a solid foundation in hospitality, completing my training as an assistant in hotel- and tourism management at the School of Young Tourism in Freilassing. Since 2014, I’ve been a flight attendant with Deutsche Lufthansa, embracing my love for travel and meeting people from around the world. In 2019, I decided to expand my horizons further by pursuing a Bachelor’s degree in Media Informatics through a distance learning program at IU International University.

When I’m not flying or studying, I love playing the E-bass, attending concerts, capturing moments through photography, or planning my next diving adventure. Thanks for stopping by!
